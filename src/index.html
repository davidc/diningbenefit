Dining Benefit

This map is designed to aid you in utilising your dining benefit by providing information on participating restaurants in a more useful format.

It is up to you to verify that the restaurant you select is actually a current participant of the program using the official list.

Current limitations:

  1. Currently may only show restaurant eligibility for to UK cardmembers (to be checked).
  2. Currently shows countries where your benefit may not actually be usable due to isVisibleToOtherMarkets being false (to be resolved together with #1)
  3. Many restaurants were not geocoded by Amex so I have attempted to geocode them with Nominatim, however:
    1. Some restaurants have been geocoded entirely wrong - look at Africa and Uruguay for example
    2. 164 participating restaurants are currently not geocoded at all and do not appear on the map
